About PT. Ascarya Prima Perkasa

Committed to Excellence in CCTV Maintenance and Cleaning Services

PT. Ascarya Prima Perkasa is a leading provider of professional CCTV maintenance and cleaning services, dedicated to ensuring 24/7 surveillance and pristine environments. Established with a vision to deliver top-notch security and hygiene solutions, we manage over 8,264 CCTV locations and provide high-quality cleaning for offices and ATM booths across the region.

  • Over 8,264 CCTV locations managed with real-time monitoring
  • Supported by more than 400 trained technicians and cleaning staff
  • Eco-friendly cleaning solutions with Pasti Clean app tracking

Our mission is to enhance security and cleanliness through innovative technology, including the Pasti CCTV and Pasti Clean apps, ensuring peace of mind for our clients.

Our Services

Professional solutions for CCTV maintenance and cleaning services

CCTV Maintenance

Ensure 24/7 surveillance with periodic cleaning, component maintenance, and real-time monitoring via Pasti CCTV app.

Cleaning Services

High-quality cleaning for offices and ATM booths with eco-friendly tools and Pasti Clean app for real-time tracking.

Custom Solutions

Tailored maintenance and cleaning services to meet specific client needs with professional support.
